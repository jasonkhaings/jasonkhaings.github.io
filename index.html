What is EdTech?

EdTech, or Educational Technology, refers to the use of technology to enhance learning and teaching. It includes tools like online platforms, mobile apps, and software that improve access to education.

The Impact of EdTech in Myanmar

During the COVID-19 pandemic and the military coup, traditional schooling faced significant disruptions. EdTech emerged as a lifeline, enabling remote learning and providing access to vital resources for students and teachers alike. It continues to play a crucial role in bridging educational gaps across the country.
