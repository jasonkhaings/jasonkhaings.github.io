A Bridge to Education for Refugee Students

Testimony: "I never thought I could go back to school after leaving my home due to conflict. With online classes, I found hope again. I now have the skills to work in a local NGO and support others like me." says Kyaw Thuta

For students displaced by political unrest, EdTech platforms have provided a second chance. Refugees and internally displaced students gained access to literacy programs and university preparation courses, allowing them to rebuild their lives and communities.

Breaking Barriers for Rural Girls

Testimony: "In our village, education stopped at primary school. Thanks to mobile apps and eLearning, I’ve learned English and can now train as a healthcare worker." says Aung Oo

EdTech tools have empowered girls in remote regions to achieve their dreams, offering them resources to break free from traditional barriers and gain professional skills for brighter futures.

Thriving During the Pandemic

Testimony: "When schools closed during COVID-19, I thought my education was over. With Zoom classes and online exams, I stayed on track and even scored higher than I expected." says Su Myat Noe

EdTech provided uninterrupted learning opportunities during school closures, equipping students with the tools to excel academically despite challenges posed by the pandemic.

Career Pathways Through Digital Literacy

Testimony: "I took an online digital marketing course while working as a farm laborer. Now, I manage a small business, helping others bring their products to a larger audience." says Thiri Shwe

By learning digital skills through EdTech platforms, students have transitioned into promising careers, from IT support to entrepreneurial ventures, improving their lives and contributing to economic development.

Inclusivity for Marginalized Groups

Testimony: "As a visually impaired student, I struggled to keep up in traditional schools. Now, eLearning platforms with audio lessons and screen readers help me study effectively." says Agga Myo

Inclusive EdTech programs are transforming the lives of students with disabilities by offering tailored learning solutions, making education more accessible than ever before.
